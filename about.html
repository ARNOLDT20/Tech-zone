Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Arnold Tarimo, T20 CLASSIC Tech began as a specialized automation studio focused on WhatsApp business solutions. Recognizing the growing need for affordable AI tools in Tanzania's emerging tech ecosystem, we've rapidly expanded our service offerings.

Today, we combine local market understanding with international-grade technical expertise to deliver bots, APKs, and cloud solutions that drive real business impact.
